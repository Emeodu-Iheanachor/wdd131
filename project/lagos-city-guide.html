Lagos City Guide – Website Planning Document

Site Name

Lagos City Guide

The name was chosen because the website focuses on providing visitors and residents with helpful information about Lagos city, including transportation, food, attractions, and local tips.

Optional domain: lagoscityguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as an informational guide for people who want to explore Lagos. The website will provide details about transportation options, popular food and dining locations, tourist attractions, and essential travel tips.

Scenarios

  • What is the best way to move around Lagos as a first-time visitor?
  • Where can I find popular local restaurants and street food in Lagos?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Blue (#0A3D62) – Used for headings, header background

Secondary Color: Gold (#F5A623) – Used for accents, borders, highlights

Typography

Poppins – Used for headings and body text to maintain a clean, modern, and readable appearance across devices.
